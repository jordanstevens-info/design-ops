# /pd-scope

Create a designer-owned scope artifact.

## Arguments

- `topic`: optional problem, feature, Jira key, Figma URL, branch, or notes
- `type`: optional hint: `design`, `research`, `qa`, `competitive`, or `team`

## Workflow

Route to `product-design-workflow` in scope mode. Use `.design/` if present, but do not require bootstrap.

## Output

A shareable design scope, research plan, QA plan, competitive-analysis brief, or team-facing design plan.
