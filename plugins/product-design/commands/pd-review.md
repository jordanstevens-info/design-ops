# /pd-review

Review a design artifact or branch.

## Arguments

- `target`: MR/PR URL, branch, ticket, prototype, Figma URL, screenshot, or notes

## Workflow

Route to `product-design-workflow` in review mode. Classify the target first: MR, branch, ticket, prototype, Figma/static design, or another designer's work. If ambiguous, ask for the target type.

## Output

Findings ordered by severity, missing evidence, and an acceptance/readiness/critique summary appropriate to the target.
