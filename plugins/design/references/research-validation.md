# Research And Validation

Research gathers evidence. Validation checks whether an artifact works for the
people and decision it was created for. Neither is the same as design review.

## Research Packet

Use for scope, prototype-first work, or missing-evidence follow-up:

- Research question
- Assumption or risk being tested
- Target users or stakeholder groups
- Method
- Evidence needed
- Existing evidence
- Gaps
- Decision this research should inform

Methods can include secondary research, competitive analysis, heuristic
analysis, analytics review, stakeholder interviews, user interviews,
observation, workshops, or usability testing.

AI can draft plans, synthesize approved source material, compare competitors,
summarize approved non-call-recording transcripts, and cluster findings. Exclude
C4, call recordings, unapproved third-party IP, and any source whose rights or
classification are unclear. Humans own facilitation, qualitative interpretation,
and final design decisions.

## Validation Plan

Use when an artifact needs evidence before advancing:

- Artifact under test
- Assumptions being tested
- Participant or reviewer group
- Method
- Tasks or prompts
- Evidence needed
- Existing evidence
- Decision this should inform
- Confidence target
- Data handling notes
- Stop condition

## Validation Findings

Use after evidence has been gathered or inspected:

- Observations
- Quantitative signals when available
- Qualitative interpretation
- Evidence quality
- Confidence
- Decision impact
- Artifact changes recommended
- Next evidence needed
- Feedback report or rerun path

Do not treat AI persona testing as user validation. It is diagnostic only.
