# PM Plugin Seams

`pd-workflow` complements product management workflows. It does not replace
them.

## Product Design Owns

- Design scope and non-goals
- Research plan for design decisions
- QA plan for design acceptance
- Competitive-analysis brief focused on design exploration
- Design story and feedback questions
- Backlog-ready ticket from design artifacts

## PM Plugin Owns

- Broad product discovery across Jira, PIF, Confluence, stakeholders, and existing initiatives
- PRDs and product requirements
- Product roadmap framing
- Full competitive intelligence
- Stakeholder interview programs

## Routing

Use `scope` when the output is a designer-owned plan to guide design work or
team discussion.

Route or recommend PM workflows when the user asks for:

- Product discovery brief
- PRD
- Roadmap or initiative planning
- Broad stakeholder research
- Full market or competitor analysis

If both are needed, produce design scope first and identify what PM artifact
should consume it.
